---
title: Hole-punchability
---

Nomad's peer-to-peer networking depends on hole-punching technology to enable device accessibility beyond local networks. When this fails, users may experience connectivity challenges.

## What the issue indicates

The system alerts users when hole-punching isn't functioning properly through a notification in the top right corner. "This doesn't mean that all connections will fail, but it does mean that the odds of failed connection are higher."

## Common causes

Network routers and VPNs frequently lack support for hole-punching protocols. The recommended troubleshooting steps: disable any active VPN service or attempt connecting through alternative networks.

## Current status

The development team acknowledges this limitation and states they're "working on ways to ensure connectivity even if hole-punching fails," indicating ongoing efforts to resolve the problem.
